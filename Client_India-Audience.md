**Target Audience Profile**

**1. Core Demographics** 

* **Age:** 22–40

*(Primary: 24–35)*

* **Gender:** All

*(Gender balance not a goal; representation must reflect actual borrower mix)*

* **Location:**  
  * Tier-1 and Tier-2 cities only  
  * Examples: Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Pune, Chennai, Ahmedabad, Jaipur, Indore, Lucknow  
* **Employment Status:**  
  * Salaried (private sector)  
  * Self-employed / gig workers with regular income  
  * **Exclude:** Students without income, unemployed, retirees  
* **Monthly Income (Net):**  
  * INR 15,000 – 50,000  
  * Core focus: INR 20,000 – 35,000

**2. Financial & Credit Profile (Critical)**

* **Credit History:**  
  * Thin-file, near-prime, or sub-prime  
  * Limited or no access to traditional bank credit cards / personal loans  
  * Has faced at least one of the following:  
    * Loan rejection by a bank  
    * Very long bank approval times  
    * Requirement for guarantor / excessive documentation  
* **Borrowing Behavior (Last 12 Months):**  
  * Has taken **at least one** digital personal loan via:  
    * Mobile app  
    * NBFC-backed digital platform  
  * Typical loan size: INR 3,000 – 50,000  
  * Short tenure (7–90 days)

**3. Digital Behavior (Mandatory)**

* **Smartphone:**  
  * Android user (≥ 95% of sample)  
* **Internet Usage:**  
  * Daily mobile internet usage  
  * Comfortable installing and using financial apps  
* **Digital Identity:**  
  * Aadhaar holder  
  * Has completed at least one digital KYC flow (Aadhaar / PAN / selfie / bank account)  
* **Payment Methods Used:**  
  * UPI (mandatory)  
  * Bank account (mandatory)  
  * Wallet usage is optional

**4. Psychological & Behavioral Traits (Brand-Relevant)**

* **Financial Mindset:**  
  * Cash-flow constrained but income-generating  
  * Short-term liquidity driven, not long-term credit planning  
  * High sensitivity to:  
    * Approval speed  
    * Simplicity  
    * Transparency of charges  
* **Trust Drivers:**  
  * App store ratings & reviews  
  * Clear repayment dates and amounts  
  * Perceived legitimacy (NBFC backing, professional UI, absence of “shady” signals)  
* **Brand Perception Factors:**  
  * Trust > price  
  * Fear of harassment / data misuse  
  * Strong aversion to hidden fees and unclear penalties

**5. Explicit Exclusions (Hard Filters)**

The following respondents **must not be included**:

* Banked, prime borrowers with active credit cards  
* HNI / upper-middle-class users  
* Rural populations  
* Users who have never taken any form of digital credit  
* Respondents recruited purely from generic online panels without verified loan usage

**6. Research Quality Requirements (Deal-Breakers)**

* Respondents must be **verified borrowers**, not “claimed awareness”  
* Loan usage must be validated via:  
  * App recognition  
  * Loan flow description  
  * Repayment experience recall  
* Surveys relying on:  
  * General population panels  
  * Social media sampling  
  * College-heavy samples

**are not acceptable**
